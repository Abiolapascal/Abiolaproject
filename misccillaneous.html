My Graphic Arts Journey

Visit My Page

In the life of designs we will always meet a major stumbling block that can either cause us to give up or gain vast motivation in doing the best we can no matter what. It was hard at first for me to branch over from being an artist to becoming a designer. It took me years of practice and endurance to expand my mind from doing artwork which pleases me to doing what the client want. It took lots of criticism and tears to reach the placei am at today where some how you can look at your client's ora and personality and just know what design they would like without them even telling you.
