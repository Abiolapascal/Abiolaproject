Over the years at the University of Trinidad and Tobago I grew extensively in my knowledge of design and how it is best manipulated to capture the viewer's attention and interest. The number one rule is be as simple as possible at first, then when you follow the protocall of clean and easy to read then you can add flavour while keeping in mind that the main purpose of a design is to get the point across first. The fancy designs around it is there as an attention grabber, but what sense does it make to get their attention then you have no clear point to bring as to why you wanted their attention in the first place.
